SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMIZATION

Search Engine Optimisation is the difference between failure and success. I have seen through my clients that a high ranking site can generate huge volumes of leads when compared to non-optimised sites. This ultimately makes SEO services a must have. Search Engine Optimisation or SEO is the simple activity of ensuring a website can be found in search engines for words and phrases relevant to what the site is offering. In many respects it's simply quality control for websites.

A great starting point to attract more visitors to your website. SEO is crucial to helping your business succeed online as it will provide your company increased visibility while boosting your traffic. Affordable SEO that worths every penny.

SEO Services That Will Be Included With Your Bespoke Package:

  • Keywords research;
  • Speed up pages;
  • Improve titles;
  • Optimise code tags;
  • Check for broken links;
  • Site upgrade redirects;
  • Competitor research;
  • Add site to Analytics and print monthly report;
  • Add site to Search console;
  • Find and remove errors;
  • Optimised Meta Tags
  • Social Media Promotion

A solid SEO strategy combines technical optimisation, content optimisation and authority optimisation. I realise that understanding complex SEO techniques and processes requires extensive research. With SEO, Web Design, Social Marketing and Business Analyst, your website will become a success in search engines with sustained quality SEO.
